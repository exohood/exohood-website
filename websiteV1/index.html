What is Exohood’s Protocol?
ETH Compatibility, established tech stack, tools, languages, standards, and enterprise adoption.

Exohood is an open-source protocol that generates value for the decentralized ecosystem through building applications, games, services and solutions. Exohood is an organization represented by rules encoded as a transparent computer program, controlled by the organization member, and not influenced by a central government. A DAO’s financial transaction record and program rules are maintained on a blockchain.
